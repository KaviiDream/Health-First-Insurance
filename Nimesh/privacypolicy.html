privacy Policy

  1. Introduction

    2. HealthFirst is committed to safeguarding the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal data we collect, how we use and share that information, and your rights concerning your data. By using our website, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

  2. What is Personal Information?

    3. Personal information refers to any data that can be used to identify you as an individual, either directly or indirectly. This includes, but is not limited to, your name, address, contact details, health records, and any other information that can be linked to your identity.

  3. What Are We Doing with Your Personal Information?

    4. We collect and use your personal information to provide you with health insurance services, process claims, communicate with you about your policy, and improve our services. The data helps us offer personalized customer experiences and comply with legal obligations.

  4. How We Use Your Information

    5. We may use your personal information for several purposes, including but not limited to:
    • Processing insurance claims
    • Providing customer support
    • Offering personalized health care options
    • Conducting research and analytics to improve our services
    • Complying with legal, regulatory, and contractual obligations

  5. How We Share Your Information

    6. We share your personal information only with trusted third parties, such as:
    • Service providers who assist us inproviding services (e.g., cloud storage, payment processing, etc.)
    • Medical professionals and hospitals for the purpose of health care claims
    • Legal authorities, if required by law or to protect the rights of HealthFirst

  6. With Whom We May Disclose Your Personal Information

    7. We may disclose your personal information to:
    • Health care providers to process your insurance claims
    • Regulatory bodies as required by law
    • Authorized agents or service providers who are contractually obligated to maintain the confidentiality and security of your data
    We will never sell or rent your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

  7. What Information You Collect When Accessing the "HealthFirst" Website

    8. When you visit our website, we may automatically collect information through cookies and other tracking technologies, such as:
    • IP address
    • Browser type and version
    • Pages you view
    • Date and time of your visit This information is used to enhance your browsing experience and improve website functionality.

  8. Data Security

    9. We take the security of your personal data seriously and implement various physical, technical, and administrative measures to protect it from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. Despite our efforts, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is entirely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

  9. Your Rights

    10. You have the following rights regarding your personal information:
    • Access: You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you.
    • Correction: You can ask us to correct any inaccuracies in your personal information.
    • Deletion: You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data under certain circumstances.
    • Objection: You can object to our processing of your personal information for specific reasons.
    • Data Portability: You have the right to request a copy of your personal information in a structured, machine-readable format.
