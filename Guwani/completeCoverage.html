Complete Coverage

At HealthFirst insurance, we believe that health is your greatest asset, and protecting it should be your top priority. Our comprehensive health insurance plans are designed to ensure you and your loved ones are well-protected against unexpected medical expenses, so you can focus on what truly matters living a healthy and fulfilling life. Whether you're looking for individual coverage, family plans, or tailored options for specific needs, we have a range of plans to suit your unique requirements.

Plan Benefits

Inpatient Hospitalization Coverage

Full coverage for hospital stays, surgeries, and intensive care unit (ICU) charges.

Room rent and doctor’s consultation fees included.

Pre- and post-hospitalization expenses up to 60 days covered

Outpatient (OPD) Services

Coverage for doctor visits, diagnostic tests, and minor surgical procedures that do not require hospital admission.

Access to a network of specialized doctors and clinics.

Critical Illness Coverage

Financial protection in the event of critical illnesses such as cancer, stroke, heart attack, and more.

Lump-sum payment upon diagnosis of covered critical illnesses.
