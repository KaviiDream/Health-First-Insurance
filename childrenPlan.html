Children Health Insurance Plan

Safeguard your child’s future with comprehensive health coverage.

At HealthFirst, we believe that your child’s health and happiness are priceless. Our Children Health Insurance Plan is specially designed to provide your little ones with the medical care they need to grow strong and healthy, from their early years into adolescence.

Plan Features and Benefits

Extensive Coverage from Birth

Coverage starts from the moment your child is born, ensuring immediate access to critical neonatal care and treatment for newborn health conditions.

Coverage for Hospital Stays

Whether it’s an unexpected illness or planned surgery, your child will have coverage for hospitalization.

Hospitalization and surgery

Covers hospital stays, surgeries, and necessary follow-up treatments to ensure your child gets the best care possible during major illnesses or injuries.

Daycare treatments

Coverage for procedures that don’t require overnight stays, such as minor surgeries and treatments for common childhood illnesses.
